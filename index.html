The Capital One Digital for Good Promise

At Capital One, we dare to dream, disrupt and deliver a better way. Our goal is simple – bring ingenuity, simplicity and humanity to an industry ripe for change. We’re fueled by doing the right thing, pursuing excellence for millions of Canadians and continuously challenging ourselves to deliver great value.

Capital One is a data-driven technology company and we’re on a mission to use Digital for Good. We’re using our expertise and network to help Canadian charities leverage technology to further their social missions and build strong communities.

Want to find out more about what we do to drive social impact. Check out our annual Gift the Code Hackathon that creates open source digital solution for charities.
